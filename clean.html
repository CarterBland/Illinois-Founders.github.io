What is Founders Dry Cleaning?

Simple and straight: it is a service where you drop off you suits, shirts, pants, blouses,... and we take care of the rest! You can then come back and pick up your items Sunday night at the BIF.

Our Guarantee

We take numerous steps to mitigate the risk of lost or damaged clothing including: purchasing professional tagging equipment, refining our processing flow, and never storing your items in personal locations.

We will also make good on any lost or damaged items, giving you reparations up front, placing the burden on ourselves to pursue claims with the responsible party.

Our Pricing

That's right. We don't charge a delivery fee or markup for our service.
